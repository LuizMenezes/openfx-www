{heading:Bug tracking}
<p>
If you discover any bugs while using the software, please email a bug report to
<a href="mailto:dev@openfx.org">dev@openfx.org</a> with full details of the problem
and your build of OpenFX.  We will need exact instructions of how to reproduce the
bug, after which we will contact you for any extra information we may need.
</p>
