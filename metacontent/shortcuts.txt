{heading:Command Shortcuts}
<p>
Many of the actions in OpenFX are available at the press of a key instead
of through the menus and toolbars.  In addition, many of the available tools
behave differently when the Alt, Shift or Ctrl keys are held down.  This page
contains documentation on what these shortcuts are, and how the modifiers
affect the various actions.
</p>
